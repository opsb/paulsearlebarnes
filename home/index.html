Welcome to the website of pianist, organist and conductor Paul Searle-Barnes

A multi-faceted musician, Paul studied Music at Selwyn College, Cambridge and then the piano and conducting at the Royal College of Music in London. He is a frequent performer in his many roles as pianist, accompanist, organist and conductor.
