Organist and Continuo

Paul became a Fellow of the Royal College of Organists at the age of 22. He has played in many churches and cathedrals, including Portsmouth, Chichester and Salisbury cathedrals and has performed a number of times and broadcast on Radio 3 with the Bournemouth Symphony Orchestra.

For some years he was organist of St Peter and St Paul, Blandford Forum and, as Head of Keyboard at Bryanston School, played regularly for chapel services.

He has played continuo on a number of occasions with the Bournemouth Symphony Orchestra, both harpsichord and chamber organ, and recently has become increasingly in demand as a continuo player in the South of England. He has also toured Spain as harpsichordist with the English Chamber Orchestra.
